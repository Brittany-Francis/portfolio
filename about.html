About Me


Hi! My name is Brittany and I'm a software developer located in the Raleigh Durham area. I was a music educator for 10 years prior to my decision to make the jump into the tech industry, and am passionate about creating software and web applications that benefit educators. I really enjoy using the artistic side of my brain to develop eye-catching front end UI's, and the mathematical side of my brain to work on backend logistics.


When I'm not coding, some of my hobbies include playing piano, crocheting, reading, baking, and exploring the local area. I also have 3 cute cats that serve as my coding support team!
